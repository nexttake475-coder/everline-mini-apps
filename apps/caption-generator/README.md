# Caption Generator

A focused mini app by Everline Media Systems designed to help creators generate stronger social media captions faster.

## Purpose
Caption Generator transforms a simple content idea into:
- hook options
- structured captions
- CTA lines
- hashtag suggestions

The goal is to reduce the friction between idea and publish-ready content.

---

# Core Problem

Most creators waste too much time:
- staring at blank pages
- rewriting captions repeatedly
- using generic AI outputs that sound flat

Caption Generator creates a faster and more structured workflow.

---

# What It Generates

## Hooks
Opening lines designed to improve attention.

## Main Caption
Platform-aware caption body copy.

## CTA
Simple next-step prompts.

## Hashtags
Relevant hashtag suggestions for discoverability.
