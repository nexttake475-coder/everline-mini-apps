## Company

# Launch Objective

Launch Caption Generator as:
- a standalone mini app
- a proof-of-concept product
- the first layer of the Everline Tools ecosystem

The goal is not only sales.

The goal is:
- validation
- positioning
- workflow proof
- ecosystem momentum

---

# Strategic Positioning

Caption Generator should be positioned as:
- a focused utility
- a workflow accelerator
- a creator productivity tool

Not:
- a generic AI prompt pack
- a broad chatbot
- a course replacement

---

# Core Launch Message

Most creators do not need more caption advice.

They need a faster and cleaner workflow.

Caption Generator reduces the friction between:
idea → caption → publishing
